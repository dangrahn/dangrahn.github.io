You've got funding and need to turn an idea or early prototype into a real product. Every week you're making technical decisions -- architecture, hiring, vendors, infrastructure -- and each one compounds.

You don't need a full-time CTO yet. You need someone who's made these decisions before and who can drop in temporarily.
