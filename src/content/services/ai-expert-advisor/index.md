## 01 The Problem

Your team knows AI is changing everything. The engineers are experimenting. The PM is asking about "AI features." The CEO read an article about agents.

But there's a gap between curiosity and execution. Between "we should use AI" and actually shipping AI-native workflows that make your team faster.

You don't need another vendor selling you a chatbot. You need someone to build new workflows with and learn alongside -- someone who ships with AI every day.

And you won't get there with a one-day workshop or an "AI strategy" engagement that delivers a PDF. AI adoption is a practice, not a project. It requires someone close enough to your team's daily work to see where AI actually helps -- and honest enough to say where it doesn't.
